Advertising Game

Assuming there are two cafes, Cafe A and Cafe B, with a fixed total number of customers in the block. The current income for each cafe is $100 per month. If Cafe A invests $30 in advertising while Cafe B doesn't advertise, Cafe A will earn $200, and Cafe B will earn $50.

Conversely, if both cafes invest $30 in advertising, their incomes will decrease to $70 each due to the shared customer base. Now, imagine you own Cafe B while the computer controls Cafe A. Your objective is to maximize your earnings over the long term.

Hence, you need to choose the best strategy to ensure a steady stream of income amidst competition.
